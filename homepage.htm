Welcome to rare heavy metal!

This website is dedicated to rare heavy metal from the 80's (Heavy, Speed, Death, trash etc.). I have gained a lot of rare releases from bands over the years and I want to upload it to the web for you guys to enjoy!

 

About me

My name is Fredrik and i'm from sweden. I have been a metal fan since 1977 when i was 10 years old and got Love gun by KISS. when i was 14 in 1981 I heard Welcome to hell by Venom and was hooked on extreme metal. 1 year later my favourite album ever was released and that is Black metal by Venom. my favourite genres in metal are traditional heavy metal, speed metal, deathtrash (like sarcofago, sepultura etc.) and german trash metal like (kreator, sodom and destruction).
